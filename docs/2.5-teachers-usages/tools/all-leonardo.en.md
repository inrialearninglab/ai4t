## Description[^1]

Leonardo is an Australian platform for creating artistic images and videos. It is particularly used in the video game, design, and marketing industries.

## Features[^1]

* Realistic image creation: Generates detailed visuals from text prompts.
* Image animation: Transforms static images into short animations.
* Style customization: Allows generating illustrations in different artistic styles.
