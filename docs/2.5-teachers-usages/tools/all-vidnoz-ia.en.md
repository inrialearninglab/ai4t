## Description[^1]

Vidnoz is an American platform that allows generating, translating, and enhancing videos.

## Features[^1]

- Automatic video generation: Converts text into animated videos with voice-over, images, and matching music.
- Realistic voice-over: Generates narration with natural voices to accompany videos.

## Example of classroom use[^2]

A literature teacher creates a video analyzing a poem with images corresponding to the poem’s metaphors and a voice-over explaining each stanza. The video is then shared with students as an example for French exam revision.
