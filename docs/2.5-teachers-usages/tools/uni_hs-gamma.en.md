## Description[^1]

Gamma is an American platform dedicated to designing and creating presentations and websites.

## Features[^1]

* Generate personalized presentation materials from given content
* Website design

## Use Case[^2]

A language teacher proposed that high school students use Gamma App, which integrates AI and a text-to-speech service, to create and present content. The goal was to support students with pronunciation difficulties.
