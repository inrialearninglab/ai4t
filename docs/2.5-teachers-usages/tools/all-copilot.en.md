## Description[^1]

Copilot, developed by the American company Microsoft, is designed to assist with writing, programming, and managing digital content. It integrates with multiple applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, GitHub) to automate and optimize routine tasks.

## Features[^1]

- Writing assistance: Generates text and provides intelligent corrections.
- Development help: Automatically completes code in programming environments.
- Creation of summaries and presentations: Synthesizes content automatically.

## Example use[^2]
Teachers use Copilot to:
- find new approaches for lessons or projects to offer students.
- create relevant exercises for office software courses.
- develop multiple-choice questionnaires for oral and written comprehension.
- encourage students to reflect on AI stereotypes when generating videos from prompts.
