## Description[^1]

Brisk Teaching is an American platform dedicated to teachers. Through a browser extension for Chrome and Edge, it offers over 30 AI tools to simplify lesson planning, assessment, and pedagogical differentiation. It integrates directly with Google applications (Docs, Slides, Classroom, Forms) and allows generating personalized educational materials while providing targeted feedback on student work.

## Features[^1]

* Educational material generation: Quickly create lesson plans, quizzes, presentations, recommendation letters, practice tests (SAT, ACT), and more.
* Tools for specialized teaching: Create MTSS intervention plans (Multi-Tiered System of Support), IEP objectives (Individualized Education Program), and resources adapted for students with special needs.
* Administrative management: Generate emails, newsletters, and observation notes for teachers and administrators.
