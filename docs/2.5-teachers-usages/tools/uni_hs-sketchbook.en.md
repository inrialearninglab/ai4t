## Description[^1]

Sketchbook is an American software that allows creating sketches and digital artworks.
It integrates AI features to enhance the drawing experience and provide creative suggestions.

## Features[^1]

* Advanced drawing tools: Wide selection of brushes, pencils, and customizable textures.
* Automatic shape recognition: AI corrects and refines drawing strokes.
* Layer support: Enables working on multiple levels for detailed illustrations.
