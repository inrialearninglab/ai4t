## Description[^1]

Wave.video is an American platform that allows creating, editing, hosting, and sharing videos. It offers an intuitive interface and AI-assisted features to easily produce educational clips, animated presentations, or tutorials without advanced technical skills.

## Features[^1]

* Drag-and-drop video editing with integrated templates and visual elements.
* Access to a royalty-free content library (videos, music, images, icons).
