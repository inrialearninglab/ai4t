## Description[^1]

Cuenti.to is a Spanish platform that allows the generation of children’s stories from an idea. It creates stories accompanied by illustrations for young readers.

## Features[^1]

- Personalized story generation: The AI creates a story based on a few keywords provided by the user.
- Automatic illustrations: Each story is enriched with AI-generated images.
- Customization: Ability to adapt stories according to preferences or specific needs.

## Example in educational context[^2]

A Spanish teacher uses Cuenti.to to teach more vocabulary to students and introduces them to different story structures.

## Practical tips[^2]

- Review and adapt generated texts to ensure their educational relevance.
- Create links with pedagogical sequences.
