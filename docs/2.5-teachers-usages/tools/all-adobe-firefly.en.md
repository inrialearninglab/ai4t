## Description[^1]

Firefly is a web application developed by the American company Adobe, used to generate images with control over artistic parameters.

## Features[^1]

- Image and visual effects generation: Instant creation of textures, illustrations, and special effects.  
- Integration with Adobe Creative Suite: Compatible with Photoshop, Illustrator, and other Adobe software.  
- Style customization: Allows adjustment of textures, colors, and the overall appearance of generated images.
