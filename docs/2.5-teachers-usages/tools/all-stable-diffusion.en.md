## Description[^1]

Stable Diffusion is a free British web application that allows generating detailed and artistic images from textual descriptions.

## Features[^1]

* Ultra-realistic image generation: Capable of creating landscapes, portraits, and artistic compositions.
* Style customization: Allows control over the aesthetic of generated images.
* Open-source: Can be modified and integrated into different projects.

## Use Case[^2]

A French as a Foreign Language teacher used Stable Diffusion to demonstrate that artificial intelligences are not neutral and can produce results containing stereotypes.
