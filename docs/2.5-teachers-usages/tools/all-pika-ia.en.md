## Description[^1]

Pika is an American web platform that allows creating interactive videos and animated visual elements from text, images, or videos.

## Features[^1]

- Video creation: Generate videos from text, adding visuals, animations, and narration.
- Video adaptation: Modify video length, content, and format according to needs.
- Animation and transition generation: Add visual effects and transitions.
- Subtitles and voice-over integration: Create subtitles and video narrations.
