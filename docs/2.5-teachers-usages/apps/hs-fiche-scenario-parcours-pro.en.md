---
title: Teaching AI through Digital Creation
description: Introducing fundamental AI concepts through digital content creation
---

The goal of this program is to introduce students to the fundamental concepts of artificial intelligence (AI) and generative AI, with a focus on practical use in creating digital content. All elements are drawn from the forum of the MOOC "[Artificial Intelligence For Teachers](https://www.fun-mooc.fr/fr/cours/intelligence-artificielle-pour-et-par-les-enseignants-ai4t/)" - accessed 01/07/2025.

## Tools

* [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/)
  Use: Create textual content, generate ideas for product sheets, create dialogues for marketing campaigns.

* [DALL·E](https://openai.com/dall-e)
  Use: Generate images from text for visual supports such as product sheets, advertisements, etc.

* [WordPress](https://wordpress.com/) with AI
  Use: Build a website while integrating AI-generated elements (texts, images).

* [Canva](https://www.canva.com/)
  Use: Create visuals for websites, marketing materials, and product sheets.

## Learning Objectives

* Learn to use AI to create textual and visual content suitable for professional projects.
* Acquire an understanding of the ethical issues related to AI use in commerce and communication.
* Develop practical skills in digital content creation, especially for building a functional website with WordPress.
