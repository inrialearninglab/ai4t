## Main Features

- Personalized Alerts

  - Learning algorithms track your research topics and filter relevant publications.
  - Alerts adapt based on specific criteria (keywords, authors, journals, fields).

- Article Recommendations

  - AI suggests articles related to current research or defined interests.
  - Recommendations evolve based on your interactions and reading choices.

- Automatic Sorting and Filtering

  - AI reduces noise by presenting only the most relevant publications.
  - Articles can be organized by importance, date, citations, or thematic relevance.

## Cited Tools

- <a href="https://scholar.google.com" target=_blank>Google Scholar</a> Alerts: automatic notifications for new articles matching specific keywords or authors.
- <a href="https://www.connectedpapers.com/" taget=_blank>Connected Papers</a>: citation graph visualization to discover related articles and structure targeted monitoring.
