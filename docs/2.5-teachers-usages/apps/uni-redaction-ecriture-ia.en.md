They help students and academic researchers improve writing quality and facilitate citation integration using AI.

---

**[Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/)**

Linguistic and stylistic assistant to enhance clarity and coherence.

* Automatic error detection.
* Targeted reformulation suggestions.

---

**[Scrivener](https://www.literatureandlatte.com/scrivener/)**

Structured writing environment with integrated AI support.

* Organization of complex text blocks.
* Assistance in structuring long articles.
* Management of extensive writing projects.

---

**[Jenni](https://jenni.ai/)**

AI assistant designed for academic writing.

* Paragraph generation from notes.
* Automatic citation integration.
* Support for writing theses and reports.

---

**[Scite](https://scite.ai/)**

Intelligent citation system analyzing scientific articles.

* Evaluates the role of a citation (supporting, contrasting, or mentioning).
* Selects relevant sources.
* Reduces the risk of improper citations.
