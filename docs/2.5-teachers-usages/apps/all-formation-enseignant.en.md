The AI training content below is drawn from teacher experiences shared on the French MOOC <a href="https://www.fun-mooc.fr/en/cours/intelligence-artificielle-pour-et-par-les-enseignants-ai4t" target=_blank>"Artificial intelligence for and by teachers"</a> – visited on 01/07/2025.
Additional information (e.g., country of origin) was extracted from the official websites of each tool.


## [Coursera for Educators](https://www.coursera.org/)
Coursera offers online courses for teachers, including the use of AI in education. Training covers educational data analysis, classroom management, and using AI to personalize learning.

---

## [Cavilam](https://www.cavilam.com/)
Cavilam provides pedagogical resources (PDF format) to guide French and foreign language teachers in using artificial intelligence.

**Resources include**

- Introduction to AI in teaching
- How to use prompts effectively with AI
- Saving time in lesson planning
- Writing support using AI
- Using AI for vocabulary practice
- Promoting learner autonomy

---

## [AI4T](https://www.ai4t.education/)
Platform offering training and pedagogical resources for integrating AI into teaching, with practical modules, concrete examples, and tools for educators.

---

## [Pix – Training Campaigns](https://app.pix.org/campagnes/JJTQKS244/presentation)
Website for training teachers and students in digital skills, with a focus on AI use and digital competency assessment.

---

## [AI-EDUC – UNESCO](https://www.unesco.org/en/digital-education/artificial-intelligence)
Supports high schools in their digital and ecological transition. Provides pedagogical resources, training, and tools to help teachers and students understand and responsibly use AI.

**Educational Objectives**

- Raise student awareness of the educational and societal impacts of AI
- Provide teachers with tools and resources for integrating AI into practice
- Promote ethical and responsible AI use in schools

**Available Content and Resources**

- Training modules: online courses and workshops on AI fundamentals and applications in education
- Pedagogical resources: guides, case studies, examples of AI integration across disciplines
- Community space: forums and collaborative spaces to share experiences and best practices
