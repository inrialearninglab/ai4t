The tools below were mentioned by teachers on the French MOOC <a href="https://www.fun-mooc.fr/en/cours/intelligence-artificielle-pour-et-par-les-enseignants-ai4t" target=_blank>"Artificial intelligence for and by teachers"</a> forum – visited on 01/07/2025, to support learner autonomy.

---

### Quizlet – Vocabulary and Revision
AI generates flashcards and interactive tests tailored to student level. Performance tracking adapts subsequent questions to reinforce weak points.  
[Official site](https://quizlet.com/)

### Cerego – Adaptive Revision
Uses long-term memory algorithms and AI for personalized study plans. Spaced repetition optimizes learning sessions.  
[Official site](https://www.cerego.com/)

### Wordwall
Create interactive, customizable learning activities for students.  
[Official site](https://wordwall.net/)

### LearningApps
Online tool to produce interactive, playful exercises adapted to learners' needs.  
[Official site](https://learningapps.org/)

### Educandy
Transforms educational content into interactive games and quizzes to reinforce learning.  
[Official site](https://www.educandy.com/)

### Quizizz
Platform for interactive quizzes to evaluate and engage learners.  
[Official site](https://quizizz.com/)

### Kahoot
Quiz and educational games platform for classroom or remote engagement.  
[Official site](https://kahoot.com/)
