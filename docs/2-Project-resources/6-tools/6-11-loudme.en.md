## Description [^1]

Loudme is a platform for generating music and sound effects from a text prompt.

## Main features

- Voice generation: Generate realistic voices according to gender, language, accent or tone.
- Narration generation and editing: Record or import text with speed, tone and pause settings.
- Export to multiple formats: Generate audio files in MP3, WAV, etc.

## Example of classroom use [^2]

A history teacher uses Loudme to create podcasts in which he recounts historical events, modifying the tone and accent of the voices according to the context to make learning more immersive. Students listen to the podcasts at home and answer online questions based on the audio content.

Link to the tool: [Loud.me ](https://loudme.ai/)
