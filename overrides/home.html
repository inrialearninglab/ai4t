Artificial Intelligence for and by teachers

Explore and support the use of AI in education

AI applications and technologies are now an integral part of our daily lives. They are also making their way into education, with automatic text translation and dialogue with an educational chatbot, for example. The aim of this portal is to help the education community as a whole and everyone else to acquire a general understanding of AI, and to develop an informed use of AI-based toolsin the context of education.
Each resource is made available as a free, open source, multilingual resource, for free re-use and adaptation of its content in all languages.
